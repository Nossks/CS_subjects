Beneath the sky of restless blue,
a single leaf drifts, searching for ground.
Whispers of wind tell stories of time,
old as the mountains, soft as a sigh.

Rivers bend like silver threads,
stitching valleys with liquid light.
Birdsong rises in fragile arcs,
painting silence with fleeting notes.

The earth remembers every footstep,
roots cling tightly to their secrets.
In shadows of towering trees,
dreams gather like hidden stars.

The horizon burns with quiet fire,
sunset spilling over the fields.
Each blade of grass bows gently,
as if in prayer to passing dusk.

A lantern flickers on a distant porch,
its glow a promise against the dark.
Night arrives, cloaked in velvet,
carrying the scent of rain.

Moonlight spills across the stones,
turning dust into silver dust.
Hearts beat steady in the silence,
like drums echoing under the sky.

And still, the leaf drifts slowly down,
finding rest in the arms of earth.
The wind exhales a final lullaby,
and the stars keep watch till dawn.
